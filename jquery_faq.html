Facts about National Parks

Grand Canyon National Park
Known as one of the seven wonders of the world

Sequoia National Park
Home to the largest living single-stem tree in the world, the wonderfully named General Sherman.
Approximately 275 feet tall and weighs approximately 1,900 metric tons

Death Valley National Park
Stretches 5,300 square miles or 3.4 million acres

Mammoth Cave National Park
Has the longest cave system in the world with more than 3,454 mapped miles
